Data Set Description

This dataset is taken from Kaggel: Brain MRI Images for Brain Tumor Detection. The data consists of 253 images among which 155 images are tumorous (labeled "yes") and 98 images are non-tumorous (labeled "no"). An example of a non-tumorous image is shown on the left, with a tumorous image shown to the right - the tumor is denoted with a black arrow.
